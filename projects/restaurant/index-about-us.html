Our History

Located in Downtown, Fort Myers, Florida over a decade. Downtown, Fort Myers is influenced by the music and art scene with monthly festivals it is a diverse and very competitive art scene area.

Palette & Plate Gallery offers fine dining that partners with local artists to create a dining experience where the food is delicious and presented as a form of art on the plate. Where you have never eaten a better work of art.
